Here's a timeline of his life

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," inspired millions with his vision, humility, and dedication to science and education. He believed that dreams are not what you see in sleep, but those that don't let you sleep.

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from University of Madras.
  • 1960 - Graduated in Aerospace Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1961 - Joined DRDO as a scientist.
  • 1969 - Became Space Researcher at ISRO.
  • 1980 - Became the Project Director for India's First Indigenous Satellite Program.
  • 1981 - Awarded Padma Bhushan.
  • 1990 - Awarded Padma Vibhushan.
  • 1992 - Chief Advisor for the India’s Nuclear Program.
  • 1997 - Awarded Bharat Ratna.
